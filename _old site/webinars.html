I HAVE A DREAM ABOUT BADGES

16th January 2017 at 16:00 UTC

(What time is that for me?)

This Martin Luther King Day, we are collaborating with Educators Co-op to talk about how to design an effective Open Badges platform/website. Steve Regur and Amy McCammon will lead the conversation, while we will lend our expertise to add details of our experience. Join the webinar, ask questions and learn more about badges!
