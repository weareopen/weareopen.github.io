Some of what we do

The Thinkathon

We use this one-day session to help clients wrestle with their biggest problems, showing where they can get started, and how the open approaches we have adopted and developed can help solve them.

Workshops

We can help you move from zero to hero around open practices. We specialise in custom-made workshops for you and your colleagues, no matter what your previous knowledge or experience around showing your work!

Webinars

Our members are knowledgable in educational technology, community participation, open badges, philosophy, participatory design, curriculum and all kinds of other things. Join one of our webinars, or order one on the topic of your choice.

Leading Openly

We are open, lifelong learners who use open practices to make the world a better place. We're great at modeling open behaviors and bending technology to the will of open. We can teach you and your peers how to be open and agile leaders.

Community Engagement

Understanding the motivations and desires of people in your community and creating equitable and safe spaces is step one in community engagement. We can help you use open practices and the World Wide Web to interact with your networks to build strong learning communities.

We are nimble thinkers.

Our acrobatic thought processes are curious, bold, experimental. We improvise a lot, but are discipled in that improvisation.

We are connected.

We play with the new and shiny tools, but we pick up the right tool for the job. We are so inclusive that we’re eclectic.

We are a collective.

Our solidarity gives us power. We believe in surplus, not profit and work as a collective, not as individuals.

We are professional.

We are constantly learning and take a considered approach to everything we do. We strive to illuminate along the way, giving our clients a competitive edge in the world of social good.

We are participants.

We invite collaboration and aim to be co-operative despite challenges that come up along the way. We share wildly to invite brilliant people to share their ideas and feedback on the work we’re doing.
